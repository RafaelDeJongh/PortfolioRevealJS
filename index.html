Who am I?

My name is Rafaël De Jongh, and I am a Web Developer & 3D artist.

My passion in life is to develop and design websites, to always search for a new challenge and keep the focus user centered.

I started developing websites from a young age, each time facing a new challenge to achieve something new and exciting.

Experimenting with lots of different code & programming languages made me learn a lot in all these years to what I know today.

Besides that I love my hobby which is Game Development where I am specialized in creating 2D Textures for 3D models.

My main goal in life is to become a Full Stack Web Developer and keep Game Development as my hobby.

More information about myself can be found on: rafaeldejongh.com

Retro Construction Page

While I was developing my personal Portfolio, I required to have Construction Page.

This resulted in something minimalistic but sufficient enough to let visitors know that my portfolio website was Under Construction, but still providing them enough information from myself.

You can check it out on Github: github.com/RafaelDeJongh/Construction-Page

My Anime List Style

With the design concept of my portfolio in mind, I tried it out on various websites that allowed CSS customization. One of these websites is MyAnimeList.net where I made my personal list style for.

After various months in development I finally made it public for everyone to use for their own lists.
If you're interested you can get it on Github: github.com/RafaelDeJongh/MyAnimeList
